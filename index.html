How to Play:
Minecraft is a virtual open world video game where players can dig, mine, build and craft. he game is typically described as a “sandbox game” because players can create their own worlds and experiences where the possibilities are truly limitless. Unlike normal video games, Minecraft allows you to be in control of the game. Be as creative as you like!

To dig - choose the appropriate tool:

-Axe: Wood-

-Shovel: Ground and Grass-

-Pickaxe: Stone-


Bonus feature: Try to find the hidden treasure!
